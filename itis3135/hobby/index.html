What: The Hobby

Pokémon card collecting is about the artwork, the nostalgia, and the chase. You can collect sets, favorite Pokémon, or special rarities like Full Art, EX, VMAX, SAR, and vintage holos.

Common ways to collect

  • Master sets for a specific expansion
  • Favorite Pokémon or illustration styles
  • Rarities, promos, and limited runs

Who: About Me

Hi, I’m Hector. I started collecting almost 10 years ago. When I was young, I would beg my parents to buy me a pack every time we ran errands. Now I collect because I enjoy the artwork and the social aspect of trading. I take care of my cards by sleeving and topping any that I really like.

My current focus

  • Favorite Pokémon: Charizard, Rayquaza, Gengar
  • Goal: finish a small modern set this term
  • Rule: buy singles for chase cards

Tip: penny sleeves and top loaders protect big pulls without spending much.

When: Best Times

New expansions drop often. Watch release calendars for set launches, special tins, and promos at local shops. Your local shops often have certain dates when they drop, so I'd say do some research on that aswell.

Where: Good Places to Find Cards

Support local shops when you can. You meet collectors, learn sets, and sometimes get trade nights. Local shops can be very expensive though, so make sure to do your research.

Hunting Spots

  • Local game stores for singles and trades
  • Retail restocks for sealed product
  • Online marketplaces for specific cards

Local Spots in Charlotte

  • Heroes Aren’t Hard to Find: Great shop for Pokémon singles, comics, and local events.
  • Get Some Game: Known for friendly trading nights and restocks.
  • The Mighty Meeple: Hosts TCG tournaments and Pokémon community events.
  • Spandex City Comics: Fun local stop for packs, trades, and collectibles.
  • Random Fandom: Smaller store with unique Japanese cards and sleeves.

Why: Reasons to Collect

It is fun, social, and creative. You learn sets, artists, and rarities while meeting people who like the same things. I also live of the adrenaline when I pack a good card. Cards can go for a lot of money, so I know I can sell them and make profit.

Benefits
